The Shojohou Advantage

Our simulator implements the advanced modern division method, providing a deep pedagogical experience that goes beyond simple calculation.

  • Conservative Estimation: Prevents common subtraction errors by teaching safe quotient selection.
  • Revision Cycles: Real-time correction logic for overestimates and underestimates.
  • Kojima Rules: Traditional placement logic for divisor and dividend for optimal workflow.
  • Verification: Every division step is mathematically verified with the remainder theorem.
